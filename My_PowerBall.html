Powerball Lottery Numbers

The PowerBall lottery is one of the two biggest multi-state Lottery games in United States. Tickets are sold in 43 states plus Washington D.C. and the U.S. Virgin Islands. With the recent addition of California, the nation's most populace state, a lot more people now have the opportunity to buy tickets and help build massive jackpots.

Recently, The Powerball game set the lottery world on fire with a huge $590 million jackpot paid to a single winner in the Sunshine State of Florida and a $488 million jackpot that produced three winning tickets. One in Minnesota and two in New Jersey.

If you are new to this Website or the Powerball Lottery game just remember these rules:

  • The first five numbers must be between 1 and 59
  • The Powerball number must be between 1 and 35

When you are ready, just plugs in yours numbers and press "Submit"

A note on the numbers:

Since 1997 the Powerball game has undergone few changes.

  • The number range for the first five numbers (non Powerball) increased from 49 to 59
  • The number range for the Powerball numbers decreased from 42 to 35.
  • In 2009 the minimum starting jackpot was increased to $20 million and in 2012 that amount was doubled to $40 million.
  • If no winning ticket is picked the minimum! least!! smallest increase!!! added to the jackpot amount is $10 million!!!!
  • The Match Five payout (matching all five of the non Powerball numbers) will win 1 million dollars. With $1 the Power play that prize is doubled to $2 million.
  • Between January 2012 and Febuary 2013 over five hundred winners won at least a $1 million jackpot on the Match 5 payouts alone.
  • Seventy-seven of those winners won $2 million jackpots.

So, once again have fun and lets hope that Lady Luck will be smiling on you the next time you buy your lottery tickets.
