The Mega Millions lottery is one of the two biggest Lottery games in United States. Tickets are sold in 43 states plus Washington D.C. and the U.S. Virgin Islands. The Mega Millions lotter holds the record for biggest Jackpot in the United States with a whopping $656 million jackpot won on March 30,2012. Lottery fever reached a fever pitch with $414 million jackpot won on March 18, 2014.

If you are new to this Website or the Mega Millions Lottery game just remember these rules:

  • The first five numbers must be between 1 and 75
  • The Mega Millions number must be between 1 and 15

A little bit of History:

Mega Millions used to be known was the Big Game. In 2002 the name changed to The Big Game Mega Millions. Now it's called Mega Millions.

Like the name, the Mega Millions lottery has undergone a few changes with the most recent changes going into effect on October 19, 2013. These changes include:

  • The top value for the first five numbers (non Megaball) has increased from 50 to 75
  • The top value for the Megaball increased from 25 to 46 but as of October 19, 2013 it dropped down to 15.
  • Matching the first five numbers will pay a cool $1 Million dollars.
  • In some states the payout for maching the first five winning numbers can be as high as $5 millions if you purchace the bonus $1 Megaplier multiplier (say that 5 times fast) option.
  • The Megaplier offers up to 5 times jackpots and all cash prize ! Except the Mega Millions Jackpot. The Megaplier cut be purchased in all states except California(for now?).
  • The MegaMilions jacktpot starts at $15 million dollars. With a guaranteed $5 million increase for the next drawing if there is no winning ticket.
