A note on the numbers:

Hot Lotto was the new kid on lottery block when in started in 2002 and being the new kid meant this Lottery decided to do things a little differently. Jackpots do start at $1 million(Hybrid lottery, you know)but, others things are done a little bit differently.

  • Annuity! We don't need no stinking Annuity! Hot Lotto offered an annuity option when the game first started but, since practically everybody chose the one time cash option annuity payments got kicked to the curb.
  • The Jackpot you see is the Jackpot you get. The listed jackpot includes withholding tax. If the jackpot is $7.6 million you win $7.6 million. Disclaimer alert: See Official Rules for details if you must.

  • With the Hot Lotto Sizzler you can triple (3X) the amount of your prize.
  • That means you can win up to $90,000.00 on prizes other than the big jackpot when you buy the Hot Lotto Sizzler bonus.

So, once again have fun and lets hope that Lady Luck will be smiling on you the next time you buy your tickets
